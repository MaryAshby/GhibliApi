THE VEHICLES OF GHIBLI

Studio Ghibli, Inc. is a Japanese animation film studio based in Koganei, Tokyo, Japan. It was founded on 15 June 1985 and is well known for its anime films such as: Castle in the Sky, Grave of the Fireflies (the first anime to make me cry), My Neighbor Totoro, Proco Rosso, and more recently, Howl's Moving Castle and Ponyo.A popular culture education is not complete without watching their films.
